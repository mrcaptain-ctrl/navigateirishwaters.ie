Frequently Asked Questions

Our captains are qualified to operate a wide range of vessels including motor yachts, sailing yachts, catamarans, and superyachts up to 200 feet. Our extensive certifications cover both sail and power vessels in coastal and offshore environments.

Primary service areas include Dublin Bay, the East Coast of Ireland, and Irish coastal waters. Our company is based in Dublin but is available for charters throughout Ireland. For journeys to other locations, additional travel arrangements may be required.

Services are priced based on duration, vessel type, and specific requirements. Hourly rates start from €150, while daily rates typically begin at €800. Custom packages and special rates for multi-day charters are available. Contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your needs.

For weekend and summer charters, booking 4-6 weeks in advance is recommended, especially for special occasions. For weekday charters or off-season bookings, 1-2 weeks notice is typically sufficient. Last-minute availability may be possible depending on the schedule.
